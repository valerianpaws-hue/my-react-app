Welcome to Bardo National Museum

The Bardo National Museum of Prehistory and Ethnography is Algeria's premier institution dedicated to preserving and showcasing the rich cultural heritage of North Africa. Housed in a magnificent 19th-century Ottoman villa, our museum offers visitors an extraordinary journey through time, from prehistoric eras to traditional Algerian culture.
